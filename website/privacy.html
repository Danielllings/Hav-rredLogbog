1. Introduktion

Denne privatlivspolitik beskriver, hvordan Havørred Logbog ("appen", "vi", "os") indsamler, bruger og beskytter dine personlige oplysninger. Appen er en fiskerilogbog til havørredfiskere, der tracker ture med GPS, logger fangster og viser vejr- og havdata.

3. Hvilke data indsamler vi

Kontooplysninger

E-mailadresse bruges til login og kontogendannelse via Firebase Authentication. Adgangskode håndteres af Firebase Auth (vi har ikke adgang til din adgangskode i klartekst).

Lokationsdata

GPS-positioner optages KUN under aktive fisketure, og kun når du selv starter tracking. Baggrundslokation bruges kun mens en tur er aktiv. GPS-koordinater optages ca. hvert par sekunder under aktive ture.

Brugerindhold

Fotos (fangstfotos + agn-billeder), fangstdata (vægt, længde, tidspunkt, agn/flue, noter, GPS-koordinat), turdata (GPS-rute, varighed, distance, antal fisk, hændelser) og spots (navn, koordinater, kystretning, noter).

Vejrdata

Hentes automatisk fra Open-Meteo API (DMI HARMONIE 2km model) ud fra din position. Data inkluderer: temperatur, vind, vindstød, fugtighed, tryk, skydække, nedbør, bølger, vandtemperatur, vandstand, havstrøm og salinitet.

Push-notifikationer

Enheds push-token (Expo) gemmes for at sende smarte vejr-alerts. Enhedsoplysninger (platform, enheds-ID, enhedsnavn) og fangstprofil-data bruges til at matche vejrforhold mod dine historiske fangster.

Supporthenvendelser

Når du bruger support-formularen, sendes din e-mailadresse og din besked til vores support-system. Disse data bruges udelukkende til at besvare din henvendelse.

1. Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Havørred Logbog ("the app", "we", "us") collects, uses and protects your personal information. The app is a fishing log for sea trout anglers that tracks trips with GPS, logs catches and displays weather and sea data.

2. Data Controller

Havørred Logbog (Sea Trout Log)

Contact: daniellings@gmail.com

Support: support@havorredlogbog.dk

Website: havorredlogbog.dk

3. What data do we collect

Account information

Email address used for login and account recovery via Firebase Authentication. Password is handled by Firebase Auth (we do not have access to your password in plain text).

Location data

GPS positions are recorded ONLY during active fishing trips, and only when you manually start tracking. Background location is only used while a trip is active. GPS coordinates are recorded approximately every few seconds during active trips.

User content

Photos (catch photos + bait images), catch data (weight, length, time, bait/fly, notes, GPS coordinate), trip data (GPS route, duration, distance, fish count, events) and spots (name, coordinates, coast direction, notes).

Weather data

Fetched automatically from Open-Meteo API (DMI HARMONIE 2km model) based on your position. Data includes: temperature, wind, gusts, humidity, pressure, cloud cover, precipitation, waves, water temperature, water level, sea current and salinity.

Push notifications

Device push token (Expo) is stored to send smart weather alerts. Device information (platform, device ID, device name) and catch profile data are used to match weather conditions against your historical catches.

Support enquiries

When you use the support form, your email address and message are sent to our support system. This data is used solely to respond to your enquiry.

4. Device permissions

All permissions are optional. The app works without them, but certain features require the relevant permissions.

  • Location (foreground + background) — Show your position on the map and track fishing trips. Background only during active trips.
  • Camera — Take photos of catches directly from the app
  • Photo library — Select existing photos of catches
  • Microphone — Optional, only for video recording with camera (iOS)
  • Push notifications — Receive smart weather alerts based on catch patterns

5. How we use data

  • Log and display your catches and fishing trips
  • Show weather conditions at the time of catch
  • Display your trips on maps
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Analyze your fishing patterns to send relevant weather alerts
  • Generate statistics and personal records
  • Respond to support enquiries

6. Third-party services

We NEVER sell or share your data with third parties.

We use the following services to operate the app:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — User login
  • Firebase Firestore (Google) — User data database
  • Firebase Storage (Google) — Image storage
  • Firebase Cloud Functions (Google) — Weather proxy and alerts
  • Google Maps — Map display
  • Open-Meteo API — Weather data (DMI HARMONIE model)
  • DMI API — Salinity and station data
  • Expo Push Service — Push notifications

All these services have their own privacy policies. Firebase data is stored in the EU (europe-west1).

7. Data storage

Your data is stored securely in Firebase (Google Cloud) in the EU (europe-west1). Data is retained as long as you have an active account. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

8. Data security

  • Encrypted connection (HTTPS/TLS) for all data traffic
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Auth
  • Database access control (users can only access their own data)
  • Passwords are handled by Firebase — we do not have access to them in plain text

9. Analytics and tracking

We use NO analytics, tracking or crash reporting tools. There is no Sentry, Crashlytics, Mixpanel, PostHog or similar services integrated in the app.

10. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Export your data (PDF report available in the app)
  • Withdraw consent for specific permissions via device settings

11. Account deletion

You can delete your account via the app (Settings → "Delete account") or via the website. When you delete your account, all your personal data is permanently removed:

  • Catches and associated photos
  • Trips and GPS routes
  • Saved spots
  • Push tokens and alert history
  • Catch profile and weather alert settings

This action cannot be undone.

12. Children

This app is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

13. Changes
